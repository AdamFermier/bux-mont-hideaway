Well, here we are.

I decided to start this little site as a place to write things down — stories, photos, thoughts about the day — without all the noise that comes with Facebook. No ads. No algorithm. No one telling me what I should be looking at.

Just a quiet corner of the internet, and my words.

I've lived in Bucks-Montgomery county long enough to have a lot of stories to tell. Some are big, some are small. All of them are real.

Check back whenever you feel like it. I'll be here.
